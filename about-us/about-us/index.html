                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Who are Trackers Excursions?

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Trackers Excursions is a medium-sized company based in Ayia Napa, Famagusta district of Cyprus.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Whilst the company started off doing exclusively Jeep Safaris back in 1996, over the years we have evolved and today we also have minibus excursions and transfers to and from the airports and ports, while we still keep our pioneering role in Jeep adventures! We provide our guests with quality service, introducing them to unique experiences and scenic attractions, while ensuring their comfort, safety and well-being at all times.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Our Innovative Company has grown fast, broke records and set trends that others follow. We dont slow down by the fear of failure, instead, we dare to achieve whats never been done before.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  We cater mostly for visitors in Ayia Napa, Protaras and Larnaca areas. However, for special groups, we do dispatch our vehicles anywhere on the island.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  We invite you to share our vision and benefit from our expertise, professionalism, flexibility, personalized approach and comprehensive and ever-changing product and services portfolio and become part of a story of spectacular growth and determination.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Our Fleet

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  The vehicles we use for our Jeep Safari Tours are the iconic British Land Rover Defender. The reason we use these vehicles is not for the comfort but for the ability to access via off-roads places you wouldnt be able to access any other way. These 4 x 4 vehicles are for the lovers of jeep safaris and appreciation of such icons and the thrills and adventures that can be had while touring in them....

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  If this is not your cup of tea, it is not a problem as we have plenty of other tours that are done with comfortable and airconditioned mini buses.
