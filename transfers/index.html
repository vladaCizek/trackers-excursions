                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Direct Airport & Port Transfer Services from Larnaca Airport to Ayia Napa, Protaras, Pernera & Kapari

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  You’ve booked your hotel, you’ve booked your flights, time to book your transfers!

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Your journey in Cyprus from either airport or ports to hotel can often be a time-consuming stressful experience…  Let us take care of that for you!

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Whether you are a family traveling with children who don’t want the inconvenience of coaches or buses or whether you are traveling with more luggage than you can carry or you are simply an individual who would like a stress-free start to your visit in Cyprus.  Whatever the reason, Trackers can provide you with a great private transfer service direct to your holiday destination.  Arrive at your destination in comfort and style.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  We are fully licensed, our drivers are professional, English spoken and are always happy to assist you with your luggage and pushchairs if required, ensuring your safety and comfort to your destination.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  We place great emphasis on quality-of-service delivery and customer care. All our vehicles are air conditioned for your comfort, seats are all fitted with safety belts for your safety. We are big enough to cope, but small enough to care!

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Trackers are very happy to be able to offer you on line booking service so you can get a quote in a matter of minutes.  As soon as you have booked your Cyprus flight and you have your arrival information, fill in our online booking form and your airport transfer request will be processed immediately. You will then receive confirmation via email.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  We offer Mini buses depending on number of persons that are travelling, which is why Trackers have a selection of vehicles to suit from:

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  ⦁ 1-4 Persons

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  ⦁ 6 Persons

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  ⦁ 8 Persons

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  ⦁ 10-12 Persons

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  ⦁ 14-22 Persons

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  (Also Available Baby & Booster Seats)
