                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Jeep Safari Private Tours on Cyprus

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  undefined

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  “It’s all about you and your group.”

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  A private or custom tour is one where the tour is run exclusively for you. Whether you want a honeymoon for you and your spouse, quality time with your family, a few fun days with business partners or anything between we are here to plan it for you.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Our personalized service guarantees your next private tour will be unique, enjoyable and trouble-free.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  These are just some of the aspects of your tour that you can customize:

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Transport style – from 4 x 4: Land Rover Defender (up to 8 pax) or Discovery (up to 4pax)

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Tour type – Jeep Safari, Cultural Tours, City Tours, Culinary Tours, History tours, Shore Tours, Shopping tours, Heritage tours, Walking Tours even Round-Trip Tours

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Activities – from swimming, climbing, diving, canoeing to visiting museums and archaeological sites – and everything in between.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Food – simply let us know about any special dietary requirements you may have.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Tour guides and translators – we have the absolute best tour guides and they are friendly, very knowledgeable and love to show people the real Cyprus.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Group size – we take families, couples, corporate groups, sports clubs and more.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  This all means that you can forget worrying about what to do and where to go – let us sort all that out for you.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Put our team of local experts to work to build the perfect itinerary just for you. You can include some of the components of our regular tours or none at all. This is your tour; so make it your own! If you're not sure, we've got plenty of ideas for what you might want to include.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  A private tour is very flexible and personal, you can do everything at your own pace and time.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  We’ve been running private tours for years now, and have taken countless tours for families, couples, sports teams and more, and we’ve received amazing feedback along the way.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Cost vary depending on your requirements, which is why it’s helpful for us if you are as specific as possible.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Are you ready to book your private tour?

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  If so, please fill the enquiry form bellow with your requirements and we’ll do our best to make sure you get your ideal tour!

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Once Upon A Time Mini Jeep Safari

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Take a step back in time

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    A half-day, fun-packed and enlightening day out for the young and young at heart. Join us on a voyage of discovery, visiting secluded reservoirs, traditional Cypriot village, traditional Cypriot fishing harbor and many more! You can either finish this tour in a traditional tavern or on a sandy beach.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Akamas Jeep Safari

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Discover the Akamas Peninsula

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    The Akamas National Park is possibly the most picturesque corner of the island. Akamas peninsula is where you will find wild landscape, amazing natural beauty, deep gorges, and wide sandy bays. This tour offers so many more natural wonders and legends of the island than you would expect.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Taste of Cyprus

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Discover the Culinary Treasures of Cyprus

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Enjoy a gourmet tour in Cyprus, where you can taste unique delicacies, witness local customs and skills, and visit picturesque villages and traditional workshops. Lunch includes a full Cyprus meze with over ten varieties of food.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Kykkos Monastery Private Jeep Tour

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Light a candle at the holiest monasteries of the Island

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Embark on a journey to the famous Kykkos Monastery in Cyprus, where you'll witness the Icon of the Virgin Mary painted by St. Luke. Explore rugged hills, picturesque villages, and pine forests en route to the spiritual sanctuary. Enjoy an off-road adventure to breathtaking waterfalls and end the trip with free wine tasting.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Oil , Lace & Donkeys

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Take a Ride on a Cypriot Donkey

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    Join us on a short introduction discovering Cyprus culture, village life and traditions on this mini jeep safari. We begin our tour with our first coffee break at a small village where you could sit with the locals together or take a a little walk around. We then venture into the unkown up the mountains until we reach one of our magnificent reservoirs and its breathtaking mountain views.
